## 1. 문제 풀이

<br>

리뷰 평균점수는 `ROUND` 함수와 `AVG` 집계함수를 활용해서 구할 수 있다.

---

## 2. 쿼리

<br>

```sql
SELECT
    i.REST_ID,
    i.REST_NAME,
    i.FOOD_TYPE,
    i.FAVORITES,
    i.ADDRESS,
    ROUND(AVG(r.REVIEW_SCORE), 2) AS SCORE
FROM REST_INFO AS i
JOIN REST_REVIEW AS r
    ON i.REST_ID = r.REST_ID
WHERE i.ADDRESS LIKE '서울%'
GROUP BY i.REST_ID
ORDER BY SCORE DESC, i.FAVORITES DESC;
```
