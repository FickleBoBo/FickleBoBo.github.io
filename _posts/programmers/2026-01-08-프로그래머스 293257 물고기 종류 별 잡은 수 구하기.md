## 1. 문제 풀이

<br>

`FISH_TYPE`으로 `JOIN` 후 `FISH_NAME`으로 그룹핑을 해줬다.

---

## 2. 쿼리

<br>

```sql
SELECT COUNT(*) AS FISH_COUNT, n.FISH_NAME
FROM FISH_INFO AS i
JOIN FISH_NAME_INFO AS n
    ON i.FISH_TYPE = n.FISH_TYPE
GROUP BY n.FISH_NAME
ORDER BY FISH_COUNT DESC;
```
