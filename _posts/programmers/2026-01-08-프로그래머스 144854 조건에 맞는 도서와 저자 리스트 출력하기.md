## 1. 문제 풀이

<br>

`AUTHOR_ID`로 `JOIN` 후 `경제` 카테고리만 필터링해줬다.

---

## 2. 쿼리

<br>

```sql
SELECT b.BOOK_ID, a.AUTHOR_NAME, DATE_FORMAT(b.PUBLISHED_DATE, '%Y-%m-%d')
FROM BOOK AS b
JOIN AUTHOR AS a
    ON b.AUTHOR_ID = a.AUTHOR_ID
WHERE b.CATEGORY = '경제'
ORDER BY b.PUBLISHED_DATE;
```
