## 1. 문제 풀이

<br>

`WITH RECURSIVE`를 활용해서 해결했다. 0부터 23까지를 행으로 갖는 가상 테이블을 만들고 이를 `ANIMAL_OUTS`와 `LEFT JOIN` 해줬다.

---

## 2. 쿼리

<br>

```sql
WITH RECURSIVE hours AS (
    SELECT 0 AS HOUR
    UNION ALL
    SELECT HOUR + 1
    FROM hours
    WHERE HOUR < 23
)

SELECT
    h.HOUR,
    COUNT(a.ANIMAL_ID)
FROM hours AS h
LEFT JOIN ANIMAL_OUTS AS a
    ON HOUR(a.DATETIME) = h.HOUR
GROUP BY h.HOUR
ORDER BY h.HOUR;
```
